Privacy Policy — Tweetelligent

Last updated: 30 June 2026

Tweetelligent ("the app") is a tool for visually building advanced Twitter/X search queries. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information. In short: the app does not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data, and it has no user accounts.

Data stored on your device

The following are stored only locally on your device and are never sent to us or to any server we control:

  • Your search history.
  • Saved searches / templates.
  • Your friends list (Twitter/X handles, optional names and cached avatars).
  • App preferences (language, theme, your own handle if you enter one).

You can clear this data at any time from within the app, or by clearing the app's storage / uninstalling it.

Network requests

The app makes network requests only to provide its features:

  • Public profile lookups. When you add a handle to your friends list, the app requests that handle's public profile (display name and avatar) from the third-party service FixTweet / FxTwitter (api.fxtwitter.com). Only the public handle you entered is sent. This data is shown to you and cached on your device; it is not stored on our servers.
  • Opening searches. When you run a search, the app opens a standard Twitter/X (or Google) search URL in your browser or the relevant app. The app does not log in to your Twitter/X account and never posts anything.

Files you export

When you choose to export your templates, friends list, or history, the file is saved to your device's storage at your request. It is not uploaded anywhere.

No ads, no analytics, no tracking

The app contains no advertising, no third-party analytics, and no tracking or advertising identifiers.

Children

The app is a general-purpose tool and is not directed to children under 13.

Changes

This policy may be updated; the "last updated" date above will change accordingly.

בקשות רשת

האפליקציה מבצעת בקשות רשת רק כדי לספק את הפונקציונליות שלה:
